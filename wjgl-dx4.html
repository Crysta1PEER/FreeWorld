【strategy】Minecraft Caves and Cliffs is officially online, inviting you to unlock mysterious cave adventures

On January 21, the updated version of Minecraft caves and cliffs was officially launched! The "high jumper" goat in the block world, the meltable block "fine snow", and the copper and crystal that can be combined to synthesize different items... Many new creatures and wonderful blocks invite you to write a new chapter of adventure in the world of blocks, Adventurer, are you ready? Find out what new friends and surprises you will meet!


White and black sheep that grow naturally under trees

Does the "contrast cute" goat in the trailer make you curious? Goats are born in the extreme mountain biome, they like to jump around in the snowy cliff area, can be called the natural "high jumper" in the square world! During the trip, adventurers need to be careful if there are "grumpy" goats on the road that suddenly come at you. If you have the wheat he loves, Then it will turn into a "gentle little sheep" at your side to accept feeding ~

In addition to the bouncing goats, this time there are dynamic and luminous squid and cute axolotl along with the attack. The cute Axolotl is a friendly aquatic creature. Although it is cute in appearance, its actual fighting power can not be underestimated. Interestingly, it has a certain chance to pretend to sleep when it is hurt! And the glowing squid that adventurers refer to as the "moving cursor" is actually a variant of squid, When you beat the luminous squid, you also get a special drop object fluorescent ink sac.
