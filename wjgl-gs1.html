【Master guide】Block World Architecture - Country Style Hotel

Hello, everyone. This is the architect Dwarf. From today on, I will bring you a super detailed architectural guide! So that adventurers can easily build a unique beautiful building.


First of all, how to build a hotel full of rural flavor! This construction uses a large amount of wool, logs and stones, suitable for adventurers who have survived in the world for a certain period of time and have a small savings. Without further ado, let me begin to explain the building process step by step.

Build foundation & lay floor

First, you need to find a large enough open space, and then use logs in the open space to build a hollow square with a length and width of 16 squares as the foundation, and each side of the square needs to be placed four vertical logs for the next step. Since this project is to create a rustic style building and will eventually use leaves as decorations, it is recommended to use dark logs to build the foundation frame, If adventurers want to try a desert style hotel, then sandstone is also a good choice of foundation material.

This introduced hotel will be officially completed!
