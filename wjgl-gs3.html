【Master guide】Redstone Music Basics - Music Box

Hello, everyone. This is the music lover. - Dwarves! I wonder if you adventurers like to make red stone music? Today we will bring you about the most basic component of red stone music - the note box!


The note box can be activated by Redstone, making a sound at the same time, and showing a note jumping out. Adventurers can adjust the pitch of the note box with a right mouse click, and each click causes the sound of the note box to rise by a half-note a half-note is an intermediate note between two pitches, C sharp half-note =D flat half-note, for example, The black keys in a piano are the two adjacent white keys that represent the pitch. Different pitches are also represented by notes of different colors and sizes.
